About Tata Harrier

The Tata Harrier is a mid-size SUV manufactured by Tata Motors. It was launched in January 2019 and has since become one of the most popular SUVs in India. The Harrier is known for its stylish design, powerful engine, and advanced features.

Features

  • 2.0L Kryotec Diesel Engine
  • 6-speed Manual Transmission
  • 17-inch Alloy Wheels
  • 8.8-inch Touchscreen Infotainment System
  • Android Auto and Apple CarPlay Support
  • Automatic Climate Control
